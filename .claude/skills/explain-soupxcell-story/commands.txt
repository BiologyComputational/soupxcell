soupXcell Story Skill — Student Question Club Commands
=======================================================

Invoke with:  /explain-soupxcell-story [argument]

This skill explains the soupXcell math model using everyday analogies,
no biology background required. Think of it as a lightning talk at a
university math/CS/data science club.

Compare to:   /explain-math-model  (technical, formula-first, for experts)
This skill:   /explain-soupxcell-story  (story-first, analogy-first, for everyone)
